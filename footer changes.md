SECTION 1 — ABOUT
About Studiqs
Smarter Educational Decisions Through Intelligence
Studiqs is an educational intelligence platform designed to help schools transform academic data into meaningful insights that support students, teachers, parents, and school leadership.
Rather than functioning as a traditional ERP or Learning Management System, Studiqs focuses on educational intelligence — enabling institutions to understand learning patterns, identify academic risks, monitor progress trends, and support better educational outcomes through data-driven insights.

Our Vision
To make educational decision-making more informed, proactive, and meaningful for every student, teacher, parent, and institution.

Our Mission
To simplify educational analytics for schools. 
To help educators identify opportunities for early intervention and support. 
To provide parents with greater visibility into student progress. 
To enable school leadership with actionable academic intelligence. 
To promote data-informed educational decisions across institutions. 

What Makes Studiqs Different
Studiqs is built around educational intelligence rather than administrative management.
The platform focuses on:
Academic performance intelligence 
Learning trend analysis 
Risk identification and early intervention indicators 
Topic and concept understanding analysis 
Institutional performance insights 
Actionable educational recommendations 

Designed For
Studiqs is designed to support:
